## Technical Details

- The `stripe_event_id` unique-or-null is the cornerstone of idempotency. Use `unique=True, null=True` on the field; Django allows multiple nulls but enforces uniqueness on non-null values (in Postgres, the default).
- WebhookEvent is a separate table from Payment because not every event creates a Payment row (e.g., `account.updated`). The WebhookEvent table is the universal audit.
- `Booking.balance_amount` is a property, not a field. v8 may or may not have landed; the helper handles both cases.
